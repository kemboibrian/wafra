About Us Protecting What Matters Most

Wafra Insurance Agencies Limited is an insurance broker / intermediary offering client-centered solutions in Kenya. Through partnerships with licensed insurers, we provide access to motor, property, health, and business insurance.

We value integrity, responsiveness, and transparency in serving our clients. While we strive for prompt and fair claims support, settlements are handled by the respective insurers according to policy terms.

  • Access to trusted insurers — Jubilee, ICEA Lion, Monarch, Trident & APA
  • Responsive and transparent claims support
  • Trusted by clients in Nairobi, Northern, and Southern Kenya
  • Personalized, client-focused service
  • Ethical and transparent operations
